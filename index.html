NUIT DE LA LECTURE

The purpose was to make the official website of the event Nuit de la lecture 2018. This site should contain all the information of this event as well as promote the organizing region. We find an interactive map on the homepage which allows to filter according to the theme and the reading supports as well as to choose its library according to the activties.

DAY/NIGHT PROJECT

During this project, we had to think about a innovative concept and make an unique interface. Plus,the project should come from the combination of three themes : time, space and interface design. My project is about the constrast of classic and night mode in interfaces and help for time zone. With the map, you can add a lot of people from various places and see instantly if they are available or not based on the time and it's representation on the map (day/night background). A video demo is available here.

EGG TRACKER

This work is my final model of an exercice : create an iOS app that control and display the information of your smart objects. Theses smart objects were here to count the number of eggs in a chicken house and the app displayed the numbers and do statistics to inform you about the evolution of your chicken house. Plus, it could alert you when something is wrong.
